# Oreo Chocolate Shake

## Ingredients:
- Milkshake premix – 1 packet
- Ice cubes – 3–4 pieces (60-70g)
- Chocolate syrup – 20–25 ml
- Milk – 150 ml
- Oreo biscuits – 3 pieces
- Oreo biscuit – 1 piece (for garnishing)

## Method:
1. Take milk and ice cubes in a mixer jar.
2. Add milkshake premix and Oreo biscuits.
3. Grind well until smooth.
4. Decorate the glass with chocolate syrup and pour the shake.
5. Garnish with an Oreo biscuit on top.

## Serving suggestions:
- **For dine-in:** Serve in a Mojito/Shake glass (300 ml).
- **For parcel:** Use a Mojito/Shake glass (300 ml), cover with Black cap.
