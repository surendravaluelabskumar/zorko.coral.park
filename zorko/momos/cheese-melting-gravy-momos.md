# Cheese Melting Gravy Momos

## Ingredients:
- Fried Momos – 6 pcs
- Barbeque Sauce – 30 g
- White Creamy Sauce – 30 g
- Global Sauce – 30 g
- Processed Cheese – 20 g (grated)

## Method:
1. Microwave frozen momos for 30-60 seconds, then deep fry until golden brown.
2. Place on a microwave-safe plate.
3. On fried momos add 30 g barbeque sauce.
4. add Global sauce on each.
5. Drizzle white creamy sauce and sprinkle grated cheese on top.
6. Microwave again for 30 seconds until cheese melts.
7. Serve hot on ZORKO butter paper placed on a quarter plate.

## Serving Instructions:
### For Dine-In:
- Serve on a quarter plate lined with ZORKO Butter Paper.
### For Parcel:
- Pack in the momos container.
