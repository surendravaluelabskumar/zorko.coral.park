# Fried Momos

## Ingredients:
- Frozen Momos – 6 pcs
- Barbeque Sauce – 15 g
- White Creamy Sauce – 15 g
- Peri Peri Masala – 1 pinch

## Method:
1. Microwave 6 frozen momos for 30-60 seconds.
2. Deep fry the momos until golden and crispy.
3. Place on ZORKO butter paper on a quarter plate.
4. Sprinkle peri peri masala on top.
5. Serve with barbeque sauce and white creamy sauce dip.

## Serving Instructions:
### For Dine-In:
- Serve on a quarter plate lined with ZORKO Butter Paper.
