# Royal Paneer Grilled Burger

## Ingredients:
- Burger Bun – 1 no.
- Global Sauce – 20-25 g
- Universal Sauce – 20-25 g
- Onion – 3–4 slices
- Tomato – 2–3 slices
- Lettuce – 20-25 g
- Red Paprika – 4–5 pcs
- Peri Peri Masala – 5 g
- Amul Fried Paneer – 100 g (cut into 2 pieces)
- Butter – 10 g
- Salt – to taste
- Peri Peri Powder – as required
- Korean Sauce – 15 g
- Global Sauce – 15 g

## Method:
1. Cut the 100 g Amul Fried Paneer block into two equal slices.
2. Grill both pieces in a sandwich griller until golden.
3. In a pan, add butter (10 g), and saute both grilled paneer pieces with salt and Peri Peri powder for about 10 seconds only. Remove and keep aside.
4. Cut the burger bun into halves.
5. Apply 25 g Global Sauce on the bottom bun and 25 g Universal Sauce on the top bun.
6. On the bottom bun, place 3–4 onion slices, 2–3 tomato slices, 20–25 g lettuce, 4–5 pcs red paprika.
7. Sprinkle with Peri Peri Masala (5 g).
8. Place one grilled paneer slice on top of the veggies.
9. Drizzle 15 g Korean Sauce over the paneer.
10. Add the second grilled paneer slice.
11. Drizzle 15 g Global Sauce over the top paneer slice.
12. Close the burger with the top bun.

## Serving Instructions:
### For Dine-In:
- Microwave the burger for 10 seconds.
- Cut into two halves.
- Apply butter on top for garnishing.
### For Parcel:
- No butter application.
- Wrap and pack using ZORKO Butter Paper only.
