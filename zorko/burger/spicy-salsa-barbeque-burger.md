# Spicy Salsa Barbeque Burger

## Ingredients:
- Burger Bun – 1 no.
- White Creamy Sauce – 20-25 g
- Salsa Sauce – 60 g (30 g each on both sides)
- Barbeque Sauce –20-25g
- Lettuce – 20-25 g
- Peri Peri Masala – as required
- Fried Burger Tikki – 1 no.
- Global Sauce – 15-20 g

## Method:
1. Cut the burger bun into two halves.
2. On the bottom side, apply White Creamy Sauce (25 g).
3. On the upper side, apply Barbeque Sauce (25 g).
4. Apply Salsa Sauce (30 g) on both sides of the bun.
5. Place 20–25 g Lettuce on the bottom bun.
6. Sprinkle Peri Peri Masala over the lettuce.
7. Place the Fried Burger Tikki on top.
8. Drizzle Global Sauce (20 g) over the tikki.
9. Close and assemble the burger.

## Serving Instructions:
### For Dine-In:
- Microwave the burger for 15 seconds before serving.
- Do not cut the burger into pieces.
### For Parcel:
- No microwaving.
- No butter application.
- Pack using ZORKO Butter Paper only.
