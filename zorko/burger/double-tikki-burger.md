# Double Tikki Burger

## Ingredients:
- Burger Bun – 1 no.
- Burger Sauce – 40 g
- White Creamy Sauce – 20-25 g
- Onion – 2 slices
- Tomato – 2 slices
- Lettuce – 20-25 g
- Peri Peri Powder – as required
- Fried Burger Tikki – 2 pcs.

## Method:
1. Cut the burger bun into two halves.
2. Apply 20–25 g Creamy Sauce on the upper bun.
3. Apply White Creamy Sauce (25 g) on the lower bun.
4. On the bottom bun, place 2 onion slices and 2 tomato slices.
5. Add 20–25 g lettuce over them.
6. Sprinkle Peri Peri Powder as required.
7. Place the 1st Fried Tikki, then apply 15–20 g Burger Sauce over it.
8. Add the 2nd Fried Tikki, and then assemble the burger.

## Serving Instructions:
### For Dine-In:
- Microwave the burger for 15 seconds before serving.
- Cut into 2 pieces.
- Apply butter on top for garnishing.
### For Parcel:
- No microwaving.
- No butter application.
- Pack using ZORKO Butter Paper only.
