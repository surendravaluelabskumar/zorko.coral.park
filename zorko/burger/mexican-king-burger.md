# Mexican King Burger

## Ingredients:
- Burger Bun – 1 no.
- Burger Sauce – 20-25 g
- White Creamy Sauce – 20-25 g
- Onion – 2 slices
- Tomato – 2 slices
- Peri Peri Powder – as required
- Jalapeno – 3-4 pcs
- Sweet Corn – 8-10 pcs
- Barbeque Sauce – 10–12 g
- Orange Cheese Sauce – 10–12 g
- Pizza Sauce – 10–12 g
- Fried Burger Tikki – 1 pc

## Method:
1. Cut the burger bun into two halves.
2. Apply Burger Sauce on the upper bun and White Creamy Sauce on the lower bun.
3. On the bottom bun, add 2 onion slices, 2 tomato slices, and 20–25 g lettuce.
4. Sprinkle with Peri Peri Powder.
5. Add 3-4 jalapenos and 8-10 sweet corn kernels.
6. Drizzle Barbeque Sauce (10–12 g) and Orange Cheese Sauce (10–12 g) over the bottom side.
7. Place the fried tikki over this.
8. Spread Pizza Sauce (10–12 g) on top of the tikki.
9. close the burger with the top bun.

## Serving Instructions:
### For Dine-In:
- Microwave for 10 seconds before serving.
- Cut into 2 pieces.
- Apply butter on top for garnishing.
### For Parcel:
- No microwaving.
- No butter application.
- Wrap using ZORKO Butter Paper only.
