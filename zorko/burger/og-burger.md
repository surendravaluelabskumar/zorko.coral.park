# Classic OG Burger

## Ingredients:
- Burger Bun – 1 no.
- Burger Sauce – 20-25 g
- White Creamy Sauce – 20-25 g
- Onion – 2 slices
- Tomato – 2 slices
- Lettuce – 20-25 g
- Peri Peri Powder – 1 g
- Fried Burger Tikki – 1 no.

## Method:
1. Cut the burger bun into two halves.
2. Apply Burger Sauce on the upper half and White Creamy Sauce on the lower half.
3. Place 2 slices of onion, followed by 2 slices of tomato.
4. Add 20–25 g lettuce.
5. Sprinkle with Peri Peri powder.
6. Add the fried burger tikki and assemble the burger.

## Serving Instructions:
### For Dine-In:
- Microwave the assembled burger for 15 seconds.
- Cut into two halves.
- Apply butter on top for garnishing.
### For Parcel:
- Do not microwave.
- Do not apply butter.
- Pack using ZORKO Butter Paper only.
