# Korean Burger

## Ingredients:
- Burger Bun – 1 no.
- Korean Sauce – 20-25 g
- White Creamy Sauce – 20-25g
- Onion – 2 slices
- Tomato – 2 slices
- Lettuce – 20-25 g
- Peri Peri Powder – 1 g
- Jalapeno – 4-5 pcs
- Fried Burger Tikki – 1 no.

## Method:
1. Cut the burger bun into two halves.
2. Apply Korean Sauce on the top half and White Creamy Sauce on the bottom half.
3. Place 2 slices of onion, followed by 2 slices of tomato.
4. Add Jalapeno on it.
5. Add lettuce (20-25 g).
6. Sprinkle a pinch of Peri Peri Powder over the veggies.
7. Assemble the burger.

## Serving Instructions:
### For Dine-In:
- Microwave the assembled burger for 15 seconds.
- Cut into two halves.
- Apply butter on top for visual appeal and taste.
- Serve on ZORKO Butter Paper.
### For Parcel:
- Do not microwave.
- Do not apply butter.
- Wrap securely using ZORKO Butter Paper only.
