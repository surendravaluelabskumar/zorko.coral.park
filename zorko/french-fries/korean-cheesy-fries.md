# Korean Cheesy Fries

## Ingredients:
- French Fries – 160 g
- Salt – as required
- Korean Sauce – 30 g
- Orange Cheese Sauce – 30 g

## Method:
1. Preheat the fryer before starting (this ensures fries cook evenly).
2. Fry 160 g French Fries until crispy and golden brown.
3. Transfer the hot fries to a dry mixing bowl.
4. Add salt as needed.
5. Add Fries on Butter Paper and spread evenly.
6. Drizzle Korean Sauce (30 g) and Orange Cheese Sauce (30 g) over the fries.

## Serving Instructions:
### For Dine-In:
- Serve in the ZORKO French Fries container only.
### For Parcel:
- Place fries in the designated takeaway container.
- Cover with ZORKO Butter Paper.
- Secure with a rubber band.
