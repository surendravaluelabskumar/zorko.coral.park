# Sizzling Italian Fries

## Ingredients:
- French Fries – 160 g
- Salt – as required
- Orange Cheese Sauce – 30 g
- White Creamy Sauce – 30 g
- Processed Cheese – 20 g (grated)
- Butter – 8 g
- Sugar – 2 pinches
- Jalapeno – 4–5 pcs
- Black Olives – 4–5 pcs
- Red Paprika – 4–5 pcs

## Method:
1. Always preheat the fryer before use.
2. Fry the French fries (160 g) until crispy and golden brown.
3. Transfer the hot fries to a dry bowl and season them with salt.
4. Heat the iron part of the sizzling plate on the induction for 60 seconds.
5. While heating, make a boat shape using Aluminum Foil Container.
6. Place 2 pinches of sugar and 8 g butter on the wooden base (not on the iron plate) for sizzle aroma and slight smokiness.
7. Once the iron plate is hot, place it on the wooden base, then place the foil boat on the iron plate.
8. Fill the silver foil boat with the hot French fries.
9. Drizzle Orange Cheese Sauce (30 g) and White Creamy Sauce (30 g) in a zig-zag pattern.
10. Sprinkle 20 g grated processed cheese over the top.
11. Add jalapenos, black olives, and red paprika evenly for flavor and garnish.

## Serving Instructions:
### For Dine-In:
- Serve the platter with the foil boat on the sizzling plate, with butter and sugar placed on the wooden base below for aroma and effect.
- Present it with ZORKO Butter Paper underneath if required for hygiene.
