# Salted French Fries

## Ingredients:
- French Fries – 160 g
- Salt – as required
- Orange Cheese Sauce Dip – 20 g

## Method:
1. Preheat the fryer before use. *(Always preheat the fryer before frying the French fries.)*
2. Fry the French Fries (160 g) in the fryer until they are slightly crispy.
3. Remove the fries into a dry bowl, season with salt.
4. Serve with 20 g Orange Cheese Sauce Dip on the side.

## Serving Instructions:
### For Dine-In:
- Serve in the ZORKO French Fries container only.
### For Parcel:
- Place fries in the fries container.
- Cover with ZORKO Butter Paper.
- Use a rubber band to secure the pack.
