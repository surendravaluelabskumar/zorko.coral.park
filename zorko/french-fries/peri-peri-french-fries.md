# Peri Peri French Fries

## Ingredients:
- French Fries – 160 g
- Salt – as required (in good quantity)
- Peri Peri Masala – as required (in good quantity)
- Orange Cheese Sauce Dip – 20 g

## Method:
1. Preheat the fryer before frying. *(Always preheat the fryer before frying the French fries.)*
2. Fry the French Fries (160 g) until crispy and golden brown.
3. Transfer the fries into a dry bowl.
4. Season with salt and toss well with Peri Peri Masala in good quantity.
5. Serve with 20 g Orange Cheese Sauce Dip on the side.

## Serving Instructions:
### For Dine-In:
- Serve in the ZORKO French Fries container only.
### For Parcel:
- Place fries in the designated container.
- Cover with ZORKO Butter Paper.
- Secure with a rubber band.
