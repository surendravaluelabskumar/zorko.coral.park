# Strawberry Mojito

## Ingredients:
- Mint Leaves – 10-12 medium-sized leaves
- Strawberry Syrup – 20-25 ml
- Lemon Slice – 1 round slice (for garnish)
- Lemon flavored Soda – 250 ml
- Ice cube - 1

## Method:
1. Take a 300 ml glass.
2. Add 10-12 mint leaves and gently crush them to release flavor.
3. Pour in 20-25 ml of strawberry syrup.
4. Add 1 lemon slice for garnish.
5. Pour 250 ml of lemon flavored soda into the glass.
6. Add Ice cube.
7. Stir well and serve chilled.

## Serving Style:
- **Dine-in:** Serve in a 300 ml mojito/shake glass.
- **Parcel:** Use a 300 ml mojito/shake glass, cover with black cap.
