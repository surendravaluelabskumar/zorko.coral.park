# Rose Petal Mojito

## Ingredients:
- Rose syrup – 30-35 ml
- Mint leaves – 10-12
- Lemon juice – from ½ lemon
- Lemon slice – 1 round slice (for garnish)
- Lemon flavored soda- 250 ml
- Salt – pinch
- Ice cube - 1

## Method:
1. Take a glass and add mint leaves and a pinch of salt.
2. Muddle gently until the mint is crushed.
3. Add half a lemon’s juice, followed by rose syrup and ice.
4. Pour lemon flavored soda (250 ml) into the glass.
5. Add the lemon slice and mix well.
6. Serve chilled.

## Serving suggestions:
- **For dine-in:** Serve in a Mojito/Shake glass (300 ml).
- **For parcel:** Use a Mojito/Shake glass (300 ml), cover with Black cap.
