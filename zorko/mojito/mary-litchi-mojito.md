# Mary Litchi Mojito

## Ingredients:
- Litchi syrup – 20-25 ml
- Mint leaves – 10-12 medium-sized
- Lemon slice – 1 round slice (for garnish)
- Lemon flavored soda- 250 ml
- Ice cube - 1

## Method:
1. Take a glass and add mint leaves. Gently crush the mint to release its flavor.
2. Add Litchi syrup to the glass.
3. Place a lemon slice inside.
4. Pour lemon flavored soda (250 ml) into the glass.
5. Add Ice cube
6. Mix well and serve chilled.

## Serving Style:
- **Dine-in:** Serve in a 300 ml mojito/shake glass.
- **Parcel:** Use a 300 ml mojito/shake glass, cover with black cap.
