# Veg Exotica Pizza

## Ingredients:
- Pizza Base – 1 (7")
- Pizza Sauce – 35-40 g
- White Creamy Sauce – 20 g
- Mozzarella Cheese – 30–35 g
- Onion – (Juliane, approx. 9-10 pieces)
- Green Capsicum – (approx. 9-10 pieces)
- Jalapeño – 10 g (approx. 4 pcs)
- Olives – 10 g (approx. 4 pcs)
- Red Paprika – 10 g (approx. 4 pcs)
- Pizza Seasoning – 2 g
- Peri Peri Masala – as required (for garnish after baking)

## Method:
1. Spread Pizza Sauce (35-40 g) evenly over the pizza base.
2. Drizzle White Creamy Sauce (20 g) in a zig-zag pattern over the sauce.
3. Add Onion – 5–6 pcs, Capsicum – 4–5 pcs.
4. Add Mozzarella Cheese (30–35 g) evenly.
5. Add Jalapeño – 4 pcs, Olives – 4 pcs, Red Paprika – 4 pcs.
6. Bake at (around 180°C)) for 4–5 minutes, or until cheese melts and base is golden.
7. Cut into 4 pieces.
8. Sprinkle pizza seasoning and peri peri and serve hot.

## Serving Instructions:
- **Dine-In:** Serve on ZORKO Butter Paper.
- **Parcel:** Use ZORKO Pizza Box and include pizza seasoning + chili flakes sachets.
