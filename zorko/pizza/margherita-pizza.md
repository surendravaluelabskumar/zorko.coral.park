# Margherita Pizza

## Ingredients:
- Pizza Base – 1 (7")
- Pizza Sauce – 35-40 g
- White Creamy Sauce – 20 g
- Mozzarella Cheese – 30–35 g
- Pizza Seasoning – as required
- Peri Peri Masala – as required

## Method:
1. Place the pizza base on a baking tray.
2. Evenly spread pizza sauce (35-40 g) over the entire base.
3. Drizzle white creamy sauce (20 g) in a zig-zag pattern across the base.
4. Top with grated mozzarella cheese (30–35 g) evenly.
5. Preheat the oven to the required temperature (around 180°C).
6. Bake until the cheese melts and the crust are lightly golden (usually 4–5 minutes).
7. Cut into 4 pieces.
8. Sprinkle pizza seasoning and peri peri and serve hot.

## Serving Instructions:
- **Dine-In:** Serve on ZORKO Butter Paper.
- **Parcel:** Use ZORKO Pizza Box. Add pizza seasoning and chili flakes sachets as standard.
