# The Xplod

## Ingredients:
- Pizza Base
- Orange Cheese Sauce – 50 g
- Tortilla – 1 pc
- Pizza Sauce – 35-40 g
- Green Capsicum – 9-10 pcs
- Mozzarella Cheese – 30-35 g
- Onion – 9-10 pcs
- Red Paprika – 10 g
- Pizza Seasoning – as required
- Peri Peri – as required

## Method:
1. Take the pizza base and pour Orange Cheese Sauce (50 g) in the center.
2. Place a 6-inch tortilla over it.
3. (Tip: Fold the tortilla in half and trim 1 inch from both sides to fit properly.)
4. Spread Pizza Sauce (35-40 g) on top of the tortilla.
5. Add chopped green capsicum, onion, and red paprika.
6. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the toppings.
7. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C until the cheese melts and the base becomes slightly crusty.
8. Cut into 4 pieces.
9. Sprinkle pizza seasoning and peri peri and serve hot.

## Serving Instructions:
- **Dine-In:** Serve on ZORKO Butter Paper.
- **Parcel:** Pack in a Pizza Box.
