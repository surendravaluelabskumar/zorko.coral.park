# Pizza Di Sicilia

## Ingredients:
- Tortilla
- Pizza Sauce – 35-40 g
- Peri Peri Powder - 2 g
- Green Capsicum- 9-10pcs
- Onion – 9-10pcs
- Mozzarella Cheese – 30-35 g
- Red Paprika 4-5pcs
- Olives – 4-5pcs
- Pizza Seasoning – as required
- Peri Peri – as required

## Method:
1. Take a tortilla and apply butter on both sides.
2. Microwave (blind bake) for 30 seconds, then let it rest for a while to firm up. Repeat it 3 times.
3. Spread pizza sauce evenly and sprinkle peri peri powder.
4. Add capsicum and onion, then spread mozzarella cheese.
5. Add olives and red paprika on top.
6. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C until the cheese melts and the base becomes crispy.
7. **Note:** If the base isn’t crispy, cook further on a pan over induction.
8. Cut into 6 pieces.
9. Sprinkle pizza seasoning and peri peri and serve hot.

## Serving Instructions:
- **Dine-In:** Serve on ZORKO Butter Paper.
- **Parcel:** Use Pizza Box.
