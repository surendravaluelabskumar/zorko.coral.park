# Hot to Hell Pizza

## Ingredients:
- Pizza Base
- Barbeque Sauce – 35-40 g
- White Creamy Sauce – 20 g
- Onion – 9-10 pieces
- Green Capsicum – 9-10 pieces
- Mozzarella Cheese – 30-35 g
- Jalapeno – 3–4 pieces
- Chopped Spicy Green Chilly – 1-2pcs
- Red Paprika – 4–5 pieces
- Pizza Seasoning – to taste
- Peri Peri Masala – for topping

## Method:
1. **Base Preparation:**
   - Spread Barbeque Sauce (35 g) generously over the pizza base.
   - Drizzle White Creamy Sauce (20 g) in a zig-zag pattern.
2. Top with green capsicum (9-10 pcs), onion (9–10 pcs).
3. Add Mozzarella Cheese (30-35 g).
4. Add jalapeno (3–4 pcs), and chopped spicy green chilies, Add red paprika (4–5 pcs).
5. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C until cheese melts and the base turns slightly crispy
6. Cut into 4 pieces.
7. After baking, sprinkle pizza seasoning and peri peri and serve hot.

## Serving Instructions:
- **Dine-In:** Serve on ZORKO Butter Paper.
- **Parcel:** Pack in a Pizza Box.
