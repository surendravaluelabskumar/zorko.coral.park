# Korean Pizza

## Ingredients:
- Pizza Base (7”) – 1 no.
- Korean Sauce – 35-40 g
- White Creamy Sauce – 20 g
- Mozzarella Cheese – 30-35 g
- Onion – 9-10 pieces
- Capsicum – 9-10 pieces
- Jalapeno – 3-4 pieces
- Pizza Seasoning – as required
- Peri Peri Masala – as required

## Method:
1. Place the 7” pizza base on a flat surface.
2. Spread Korean Sauce evenly on the base.
3. Drizzle White Creamy Sauce in zig-zag pattern.
4. Add onion and capsicum (9–10 pieces each).
5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the toppings.
6. Add jalapeno (4-5 pcs)
7. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C until the cheese melts and base is crisp.
8. Cut into 4 pieces.
9. After baking, sprinkle Pizza Seasoning and Peri Peri Masala.

## Serving Instructions:
- **Dine-in:** Serve on ZORKO Butter Paper.
- **Parcel:** Pack in Pizza Box.
