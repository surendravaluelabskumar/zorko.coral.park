# Golden Harvest Pizza

## Ingredients:
- Pizza Base
- Pizza Sauce – 20 g
- Orange Cheese Sauce – 80 g
- Sweet Corn – 70 g
- Mozzarella Cheese – 30 g
- Pizza Seasoning – as required
- Peri Peri – as required
- Salt – to taste

## Method:
1. **Prepare Corn Topping:**
   - In a bowl, take room-temperature sweet corn, add salt, pizza seasoning, and 60 g orange cheese sauce. Mix well.
2. **Prepare the Base:**
   - Spread pizza sauce (20 g) and orange cheese sauce (20 g) evenly over the pizza base
   - Spread the prepared corn mix over the base.
   - Top with mozzarella cheese.
3. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C until the cheese melts and the base becomes slightly crispy.
4. Cut into 4 pieces and serve it hot.

## Serving Instructions:
- **Dine-In:** Serve on ZORKO Butter Paper.
- **Parcel:** Pack in a Pizza Box.
