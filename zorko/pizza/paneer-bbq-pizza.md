# Paneer BBQ Pizza

## Ingredients:
- Pizza Base
- Barbeque Sauce – 30 g
- White Creamy Sauce – 20g
- Mozzarella Cheese – 30 g
- Paneer – 50 g (cubed)
- Onion – 20 g (diced)
- Salt – as required
- Pizza Seasoning – as required
- Peri Peri – as required

## Method:
1. **Marinate Paneer & Onion:**
   - In a clean bowl, mix paneer cubes and onion with salt and barbeque sauce.
   - Microwave the mixture for 30 seconds and set aside.
2. **Prepare Pizza Base:**
   - Spread a layer of pizza sauce and barbeque sauce on the base.
   - Add white creamy sauce in zig zag form.
   - Add mozzarella cheese evenly.
   - Top with the marinated paneer and onion mixture.
3. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C until the cheese melts and the base turns slightly crispy.
4. Cut into 4 pieces.
5. Sprinkle pizza seasoning and peri peri and serve hot.

## Serving Instructions:
- **Dine-In:** Serve on ZORKO Butter Paper.
- **Parcel:** Pack in a Pizza Box
