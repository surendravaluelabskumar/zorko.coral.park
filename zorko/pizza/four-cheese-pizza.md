# Four Cheese Pizza

## Ingredients:
- Pizza Base
- Pizza Sauce – 35-40 g
- Processed Cheese – 20 g (grated)
- Cheese Slices – 2 pcs
- Mozzarella Cheese – 30-35 g
- Orange Cheese Sauce – 30 g
- Pizza Seasoning – to taste
- Peri Peri Masala – as required

## Method:
1. Spread Pizza Sauce (35-40 g) evenly over the pizza base.
2. Grate Processed Cheese, Place 2 Cheese Slices, Add Mozzarella Cheese (30-35 g), Drizzle Orange Cheese Sauce (30 g) in a zig-zag pattern
3. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C until the cheese melts and the crust becomes slightly crispy.
4. Cut into 4 pieces.
5. Sprinkle Pizza Seasoning to taste and peri peri masala,

## Serving Instructions:
- **Dine-In:** Serve on ZORKO Butter Paper.
- **Parcel:** Pack in a Pizza Box.
