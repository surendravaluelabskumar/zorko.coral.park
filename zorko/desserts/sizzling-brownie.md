# Sizzling Brownie

## Ingredients:
- Brownie – 1 piece (50 g)
- Vanilla ice cream – 1 scoop (100ml)
- Chocolate syrup – 80ml
- Sizzling plate (iron base)

## Method:
1. Place the iron part of the sizzling plate in the pizza oven and heat it for 10 minutes until fully hot.
2. Microwave the brownie for 15 seconds.
3. Pour chocolate syrup onto the hot sizzling plate.
4. Place the warm brownie on top of the syrup.
5. Add 1 scoop of 100ml vanilla ice cream on the brownie.
6. Garnish with more chocolate syrup and serve immediately while sizzling.

**NOTE:** This will not be provided in any Swiggy, Zomato, or takeaway orders.
