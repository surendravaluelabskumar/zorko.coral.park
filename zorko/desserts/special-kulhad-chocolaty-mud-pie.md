# Special Kulhad Chocolaty Mud Pie

## Ingredients:
- Brownie – 2 pieces (50g each)
- Chocolate syrup – 80 g
- KitKat – small pieces (2 sticks)
- Nutella – for garnish
- Kulhad – 1

## Method:
1. Microwave 2 pieces of brownie for 30 seconds.
2. In a kulhad, layer crumbled brownie pieces, chocolate syrup, and small KitKat pieces — repeat this layering 3 times.
3. Top with some Nutella.
4. Microwave the prepared kulhad for 30 Seconds.
5. Serve hot.
