# Cheese Chatori Maggi

## Ingredients:
- Water – 250-300ml
- Maggi Noodles – 1 pack
- Maggi Masala (from the pack) – 1 packet
- Magic Masala – 1 sachet
- Green Peas – 10 gm
- Global Sauce – 20 g
- Barbeque Sauce – 20 g
- Processed Cheese – 20 g (grated)

## Method:
1. Boil water in a vessel and add Maggi noodles.
2. Once partially cooked, add the Maggi masala from the packet.
3. Add green peas, barbeque sauce, global sauce, and processed cheese.
4. Mix well and cook until the cheese melts and the noodles absorb all flavors.

## Serving Style:
- **For Dine-in:** Serve hot in the ZORKO Maggi container.
- **For Parcel:** Use a black Maggi container.
