# Double Masala Maggi

## Ingredients:
- Water – 250-300ml
- Maggi Noodles – 1 pack
- Maggi Masala (inside the pack) – 1 packet
- Magic Masala – 1 sachet

## Method:
1. Boil water in a vessel.
2. Add the Maggi noodles and cook until semi-soft.
3. Mix in the Maggi masala from the packet along with magic masala.
4. Stir well and cook until the water is absorbed and noodles are fully cooked.

## Serving Style:
- **For Dine-in:** Serve hot in the ZORKO Maggi container.
- **For Parcel:** Use a black Maggi container.
