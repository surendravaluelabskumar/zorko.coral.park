# Veg. Masala Maggi

## Ingredients:
- Water – 250-300ml
- Maggi Noodles – 1 pack
- Maggi Masala (from the pack) – 1 packet
- Magic Masala – 1 sachet
- Onion – 20 gm
- Capsicum – 20 gm
- Green Peas – 20 gm

## Method:
1. Boil water in a vessel.
2. Add Maggi noodles and cook until semi-soft.
3. Add the Maggi masala from the packet and magic masala to taste.
4. Mix in chopped onion, capsicum, and green peas.
5. Cook until the veggies soften slightly and water is absorbed.

## Serving Style:
- **For Dine-in:** Serve hot in the ZORKO Maggi container.
- **For Parcel:** Use a black Maggi container.
