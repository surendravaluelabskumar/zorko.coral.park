# Hot Passion Spicy Maggi

## Ingredients:
- Water – 250-300ml
- Maggi Noodles – 1 pack
- Maggi Masala (from the pack) – 1 packet
- Magic Masala – 1 sachet
- Barbeque Sauce – 20gm
- Chopped Green Chili – 1-2 pcs

## Method:
1. Boil water in a vessel.
2. Add Maggi noodles and cook until semi-soft.
3. Mix in the Maggi masala from the pack and magic masala.
4. Add 20 gm of barbeque sauce and chopped green chilli.
5. Stir well and cook until the water is absorbed and noodles are fully cooked.

## Serving Style:
- **For Dine-in:** Serve hot in the ZORKO Maggi container.
- **For Parcel:** Use a black Maggi container.
