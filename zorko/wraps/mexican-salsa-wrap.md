# Mexican Salsa Wrap

## Ingredients:
- Butter – as required (for grilling)
- Tortilla Wrap – 1 no.
- Fried Burger Tikki – 1 pc (cut into 4 pieces)
- Capsicum – 20 g
- Onion – 10 g
- Jalapeno – 4-5 pcs
- Sweet Corn – 10 g
- Lettuce – 20–25 g
- Nachos Chips – 5-6 pcs
- Salsa Sauce – 20 g
- Orange Cheese Sauce – 30 g
- Peri Peri Masala – 2 g

## Method:
1. Spread Orange Cheese Sauce on Tortilla first.
2. Cut the fried burger tikki into 4 pieces and place them at the center of the tortilla wrap.
3. Add chopped onion (10 g), capsicum (20 g), jalapeno (4-5 pcs), sweet corn (10 g), and lettuce (20–25 g).
4. Add crushed nachos chips.
5. Drizzle with Salsa Sauce (20 g)
6. Sprinkle Peri Peri Masala (2 g).
7. Roll the wrap tightly and grill in a sandwich griller using butter until golden and slightly crisp.

## Serving Instructions:
### For Dine-In:
- Compulsory: Serve in ZORKO Butter Paper.
### For Parcel:
- Compulsory: Wrap with Aluminum Foil and pack in ZORKO Butter Paper.
