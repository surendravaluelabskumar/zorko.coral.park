# Grilled Paneer Wrap

## Ingredients:
- Butter – as required (for grilling)
- Tortilla Wrap – 1 no.
- Universal Sauce- 30 g
- Onion – 20 g
- Capsicum – 20 g
- Red Paprika – 4-5 pcs
- Lettuce – 20-25 g
- Paneer – 100 g
- Butter – 10 g (for sautéing paneer)
- Salt – as required
- Peri Peri Masala – 5 g
- Global Sauce – 30 g

## Method:
### 1. Grill the Paneer:
- Grill paneer (100 g) in a sandwich griller until visible grill marks appear.
- In a pan, add 10 g butter, sauté the grilled paneer with salt and Peri Peri masala for no more than 10 seconds. Set aside.
### 2. Assemble the Wrap:
- Place the tortilla wrap flat and spread Universal Sauce (30 g) in the center.
- Add the grilled paneer.
- Top with chopped onion (20 g), capsicum (20 g), red paprika (4-5 pcs), and lettuce (20–25 g).
- Sprinkle additional Peri Peri Masala.
- Drizzle Global Sauce (30 g) over the fillings.
### 3. Grill the Wrap:
- Roll the tortilla tightly.
- Grill the wrap in a sandwich griller with butter until golden and crispy with nice grill marks.

## Serving Instructions:
### For Dine-In:
- Compulsory: Serve in ZORKO Butter Paper.
### For Parcel:
- Compulsory: Wrap with Aluminum Foil and pack in ZORKO Butter Paper
