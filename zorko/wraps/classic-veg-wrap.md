# Classic Veg Wrap

## Ingredients:
- Tortilla Wrap – 1 no.
- Fried Burger Tikki – 1 pc
- Onion (chopped) – 20 g
- Capsicum (chopped) – 20 g
- Lettuce – 20–25 g
- Peri Peri Masala – 5 g
- Barbeque Sauce – 30 g
- Orange Cheese Sauce – 30 g
- Olives – 10 g
- Jalapeno – 10 g
- Butter – as required (for grilling)

## Method:
1. Spread BBQ Sauce on Tortilla First.
2. Cut the fried burger tikki into four pieces and place them at the center of the tortilla wrap.
3. Add chopped onion (20 g), capsicum (20 g), and lettuce (20-25 g) over the tikki.
4. Sprinkle Peri Peri Masala (5 g) on top.
5. Drizzle with Orange Cheese Sauce (30 g).
6. Add olives (10 g) and jalapenos (10 g).
7. Roll the wrap tightly and grill with butter until golden and slightly crispy.

## Serving Instructions:
### For Dine-In:
- Serve in ZORKO Butter Paper (compulsory).
### For Parcel:
- Wrap with Aluminum Foil and pack in ZORKO Butter Paper (compulsory).
