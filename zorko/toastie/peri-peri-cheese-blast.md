# Peri Peri Cheese Blast

## Ingredients:
- Sandwich Bread – 1 slice
- Butter – 10 g
- Orange Cheese Sauce – 30 g
- Processed Cheese (cube cut) – 30 g
- Jalapeño (chopped) – 3–4 pcs
- Peri Peri Masala – 5-10 g
- Processed Cheese (grated) – 40 g

## Method:
1. Apply 10 g butter evenly on one slice of sandwich bread.
2. Then spread 15 g of orange cheese sauce over the buttered bread
3. In a bowl, mix: 30 g cube-cut processed cheese, Chopped jalapeños, 15 g orange cheese sauce, Peri peri masala (to taste)
4. Spread the cheese-jalapeño mixture evenly over the prepared bread slice.
5. Grate 40 g of processed cheese on top.
6. Bake at 180°C for 1–2 minutes or until cheese melts.

## Serving Style:
- **For Dine-In:** Cut the sandwich diagonally into 4 triangle pieces (cross-cut).
- **For Parcel:** Place the pieces on Zorko butter paper and pack neatly in a pizza box for delivery or takeaway.
