# Italian Treat

## Ingredients:
- Sandwich Bread – 1 slice
- Butter – 10 g
- Pizza Sauce – 30 g
- Capsicum (diced) – 15 g
- Onion (diced) – 15 g
- Sweet Corn – 10 g
- Jalapeños – 3–4 pieces
- Olives – 3–4 pieces
- Red Paprika – 3–4 pieces
- Processed Cheese (grated) – 40 g
- Pizza Seasoning – 2 g

## Method:
1. Apply 10 g butter on the sandwich bread, then spread 15 g of pizza sauce evenly.
2. In a bowl, mix capsicum, onion, sweet corn, jalapeños, olives, and red paprika with the remaining pizza sauce 15 g.
3. Microwave the mixture for 30 seconds.
4. Spread the microwaved veggie topping evenly on the bread.
5. Grate 40 g of processed cheese on top.
6. Bake at 180°C for 1–2 minutes or until the cheese melts.

## Serving Style:
- **For Dine-In:** Cut the sandwich diagonally into 4 triangle pieces (cross-cut).
- **For Parcel:** Place the pieces on Zorko butter paper and pack neatly in a pizza box for delivery or takeaway.
