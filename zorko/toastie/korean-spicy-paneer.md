# Korean Spicy Paneer

## Ingredients:
- Sandwich Bread – 1 slice
- Butter – 10gm
- Korean Sauce – 30 g
- Paneer (cube cut) - 30 g
- Capsicum (diced) – 15 g
- Onion (diced) – 15 g
- Pizza Seasoning – 2 g
- Salt – 2 g
- Processed Cheese (grated) – 40 g

## Method:
1. Apply 10gm Butter on sandwich bread evenly.
2. Spread 15 g of Korean sauce evenly over one slice of sandwich bread.
3. In a bowl, mix cube-cut paneer, diced capsicum, diced onion, remaining 15 g Korean sauce, salt, and pizza seasoning.
4. Microwave the mixture for 30 seconds.
5. Spread the heated paneer mixture evenly on the bread. Top with 40 g grated processed cheese.
6. Bake at 180°C for 1–2 minutes or until the cheese is melted and slightly golden.

## Serving Style:
- **For Dine-In:** Cut the sandwich diagonally into 4 triangle pieces (cross-cut).
- **For Parcel:** Place the pieces on ZORKO butter paper and pack neatly in a pizza box for delivery or takeaway.
