# Korean Jalapeno Bun

## Ingredients:
- Burger Bun – 1 no.
- Garlic Butter – 30 g
- Pizza Seasoning – 1 pinch
- Mozzarella Cheese – 35-40 g
- Orange Cheese Sauce – 30-35 g
- Korean Sauce – 30 g
- Chopped Jalapeños – 10-12 pcs

## Method:
1. Cut the burger bun into 6 deep slices (don’t cut all the way through).
2. Apply garlic butter inside all the cuts.
3. Drizzle orange cheese sauce and Korean sauce into the cuts.
4. Fill mozzarella cheese in each slit and add extra in the center.
5. Add chopped jalapenos and sprinkle pizza seasoning on top.
6. Microwave for 30 seconds to melt the cheese slightly.
7. Bake at 180°C for 4-6 minutes until the cheese melts and bun gets slightly crispy.

## Serving Style:
- **For Dine-In:** Serve hot on ZORKO Butter Paper.
