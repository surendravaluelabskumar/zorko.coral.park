# Nachos & Salsa

## Ingredients:
- Nachos – 1 packet (45 gm)
- Peri peri – as required
- Salsa Sauce – 80 g
- Orange Cheese Sauce – 80 g
- Olives – as required (for garnish)

## Method:
1. Spread the nachos in a star shape on the plate
2. Sprinkle Peri Peri on it.
3. Add Salsa Sauce on each nacho chip.
4. Drizzle Orange Cheese Sauce over the top.
5. Garnish with olives.

## Serving Instructions:
- **Dine-In:** Serve on ZORKO Butter Paper.
- **Parcel:** Use Pizza Box.
