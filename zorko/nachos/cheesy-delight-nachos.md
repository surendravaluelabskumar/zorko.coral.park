# Cheesy Delight Nachos

## Ingredients:
- Nachos – 1 packet (45gm)
- Peri peri – as required
- All-in-One Sauce (JAIN) – 20 g
- Water – as required (to dilute sauce)
- Processed Cheese – 20 g (grated)
- White Creamy Sauce – 30 g
- Orange Cheese Sauce – 30 g
- Sweet Corn – 15-20 pcs

## Method:
1. Arrange the nachos in a star shape on a serving plate.
2. Sprinkle Peri Peri masala.
3. In a bowl, add All-in-One Sauce (20 g) and dilute it slightly with water to make it pourable.
4. Drizzle this sauce evenly over the nachos.
5. Grate processed cheese (20 g) generously over the nachos.
6. Drizzle White Creamy Sauce (30 g) and Orange Cheese Sauce (30 g) on top in a zig-zag pattern.
7. Sprinkle sweet corn (15-20 pcs) over the nachos as garnish.

## Serving Instructions:
- **Dine-In:** Serve on ZORKO Butter Paper.
- **Parcel:** Use Pizza Box to maintain presentation.
