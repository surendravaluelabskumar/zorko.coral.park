# Nachos with Cheese Dip

## Ingredients:
- Nachos – 2 packet (90gm)
- Peri peri – as required
- Orange Cheese Sauce – 20 g

## Method:
1. Spread the nachos in a star shape on the plate.
2. Sprinkle Peri Peri on it.
3. Serve with Orange Cheese Dip on the side.

## Serving Instructions:
- **Dine-In:** Serve on ZORKO Butter Paper.
- **Parcel:** Use Pizza Box.
