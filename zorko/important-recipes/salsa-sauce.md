# Salsa Sauce (approx. 289 g)

## Ingredients:
- 50 g Onion (finely chopped)
- 100 g Tomato (finely chopped)
- 10 g Coriander leaves (finely chopped)
- 5 g Green chilli (finely chopped)
- 100 g Tomato ketchup
- Chili flakes – as required
- Salt – to taste
- Global sauce – 20g

## Method:
- Mix all ingredients well.
