# Strong Cold Coffee

## Ingredients:
- Cold coffee premix – 1 packet
- Nestlé coffee sachet (₹2) – 1 packet
- Ice cubes – 3–4 pieces (60-70g)
- Milk – 125 ml

## Method:
1. Take milk and ice cubes in a mixer jar.
2. Add cold coffee premix and half the sachet of Nestlé coffee powder.
3. Grind well until smooth and frothy.
4. While serving, sprinkle the remaining half sachet on top for garnishing.

## With Ice-Creamy:
- Add a scoop of 100 ml ice cream scoop on top of the coffee.

## Serving suggestions:
- **For dine-in:** Serve in a Coffee glass (240 ml).
- **For parcel:** Use a Coffee glass (240 ml), cover with Black cap.
