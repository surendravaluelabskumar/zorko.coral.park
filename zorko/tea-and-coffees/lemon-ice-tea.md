# Lemon Ice Tea

## Ingredients:
- Ice Tea Premix – 1 packet
- Ice cubes – 3 pieces (60-70g)
- Lemon Slice - 1
- Water - 200ml

## Method:
1. Take Water and ice cubes in a 240ml Glass.
2. Add Ice Tea premix.
3. Mix well with spoon and add lemon slice.
4. Serve chilled.

## Serving suggestions:
- **For dine-in:** Serve in a glass (250 ml).
- **For parcel:** Use a glass (250 ml), cover with Black cap.
