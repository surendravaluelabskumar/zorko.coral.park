# Passion Fruit Ice Tea

## Ingredients:
- Ice Tea Premix – 1 packet
- Ice cubes – 3 pieces (60-70g)
- Water - 200ml
- Passion fruit Syrup – 5ml

## Method:
1. Take Water and ice cubes in a 250ml Glass.
2. Add Ice Tea premix and Passion fruit Syrup.
3. Mix well with spoon.
4. Serve chilled.

## Serving suggestions:
- **For dine-in:** Serve in a glass (250 ml).
- **For parcel:** Use a glass (250 ml), cover with Black cap.
