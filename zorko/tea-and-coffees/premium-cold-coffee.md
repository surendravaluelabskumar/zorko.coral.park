# Premium Cold Coffee

## Ingredients:
- Cold coffee premix – 1 packet
- Ice cubes – 3–4 pieces (60-70g)
- Milk – 125 ml

## Method:
1. Take milk and ice cubes in a mixer jar.
2. Add cold coffee premix.
3. Grind well until smooth and frothy.
4. Serve chilled.

## With Ice-Cream:
- Add a scoop of 100ml ice cream scoop on top of the coffee.

## Serving suggestions:
- **For dine-in:** Serve in a Coffee glass (250 ml).
- **For parcel:** Use a Coffee glass (250 ml), cover with Black cap.
