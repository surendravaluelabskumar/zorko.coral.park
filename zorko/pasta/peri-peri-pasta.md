# Peri Peri Pasta

## Ingredients:
(Use the same ingredients as Alfredo Pasta)
- Butter – 20 g
- Garlic Butter – 10 g
- Capsicum – 10 g
- Olives – 4-5 pcs
- Sweet Corn – 10 g
- Amul Fresh Cream – 75 ml
- White Creamy Sauce – 25 g
- Water – 50 ml
- Processed Cheese – 20 g
- Salt – as required
- Sugar – a pinch
- Pizza Seasoning – as required
- Boiled Pasta – 80–100 g
- Peri Peri Powder – 2 tsp (added after preparation)

## Method:
1. In a pan, heat butter and garlic butter.
2. Sauté capsicum, corn, and olives for 1–2 minutes.
3. Add fresh cream, white creamy sauce, and water. Mix well and simmer.
4. Add salt, sugar, pizza seasoning, and processed cheese. Stir until the sauce thickens.
5. Add boiled pasta and toss until coated.
6. Once fully prepared, sprinkle 2 tsp of peri peri powder and mix well for a spicy finish.
7. Cook for another 30 seconds and serve hot.

## Serving Style:
- **For Dine-in:** Serve hot in a pasta container.
- **For Parcel:** Use a black pasta container.
