# Ala Rosey Pasta (Pink Sauce)

## Ingredients:
- Amul Fresh Cream – 75 ml
- Water – 50 ml
- White Creamy Sauce – 25 g
- Pizza Sauce – 10 g
- Tomato Ketchup – 20 g
- Capsicum – 10 g
- Sweet Corn – 10 g
- Olives – 10 g
- Red Paprika – 10 g
- Salt – as per taste
- Chili Flakes – 2 g
- Sugar – a pinch
- Pizza Seasoning – 2 g
- Processed Cheese – 20 g
- Boiled Penne Pasta – 80 g

## Method:
1. In a pan, heat butter and sauté chopped capsicum, corn, olives, and red paprika for 1–2 minutes.
2. Add pizza sauce and ketchup. Mix well and cook for 30 seconds.
3. Pour in fresh cream, white creamy sauce, and water. Stir and bring to a light simmer.
4. Add salt, sugar, chilli flakes, and pizza seasoning.
5. Mix in processed cheese and let it melt to form a rich pink sauce.
6. Add boiled pasta and toss well until fully coated in the sauce.
7. Cook for 1–2 more minutes until creamy and smooth.

## Serving Style:
- **For Dine-in:** Serve hot in a pasta container.
- **For Parcel:** Use a black pasta container.
