# Alfredo Pasta (White Sauce)

## Ingredients:
- Butter – 20 g
- Garlic Butter – 10 g
- Capsicum – 10 g
- Olives – 4-5 pcs
- Sweet Corn – 10 g
- Amul Fresh Cream – 75 ml
- White Creamy Sauce – 25 g
- Water – 50 ml
- Processed Cheese – 20 g
- Salt – as required
- Sugar – a pinch
- Pizza Seasoning – as required
- Boiled Pasta – 80–100 g

## Method:
1. Heat butter and garlic butter in a pan.
2. Add capsicum, sweet corn, and olives; sauté for 1–2 minutes.
3. Add salt, a pinch of sugar, and pizza seasoning; mix well.
4. Pour in Amul fresh cream, white creamy sauce, and water.
5. Stir and Add processed cheese and mix until it melts smoothly.
6. Add boiled pasta and toss everything together until well coated.
7. Simmer for 1–2 minutes on low flame until creamy and hot.

## Serving Style:
- **For Dine-In:** Serve in a pasta container.
- **For Parcel:** Use a black pasta container.
