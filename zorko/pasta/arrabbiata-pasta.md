# Arrabbiata Pasta (Red Sauce)

## Ingredients:
- Butter – 20 g
- Garlic Butter – 10 g
- Capsicum – 10 g
- Sweet Corn – 10 g
- Jalapeno – 10 g
- Red Paprika – 10 g
- Salt – 2 g
- Pizza seasoning – 2 g
- Chili Flakes – 5 g
- Ketchup – 50 g
- Pizza Sauce – 25 g
- Korean Sauce – 25 g
- Boiled Pasta – 80 g
- White Creamy Sauce – 20 g

## Method:
1. Heat butter and garlic butter in a pan.
2. Add capsicum, sweet corn, jalapeno, and red paprika.
3. Sauté for 1–2 minutes until slightly tender.
4. Add salt, pizza seasoning and chili flakes; stir well.
5. Add ketchup, pizza sauce, and Korean sauce; cook for 1 minute.
6. Add boiled pasta and toss until evenly coated.
7. Pour in white creamy sauce and mix thoroughly.
8. Cook for another 1–2 minutes until pasta is creamy and hot.

## Serving Style:
- **For Dine-in:** Serve hot in a pasta container.
- **For Parcel:** Use a black pasta container.
