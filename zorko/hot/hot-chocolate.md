# Hot Chocolate (100ml)

## Ingredients:
- Milk – 100 ml
- Hot chocolate premix – 1 pkt

## Method:
1. Take milk in a pan and add hot chocolate premix.
2. Boil it properly while stirring continuously.
3. Serve hot.

## Serving suggestions:
- **For dine-in:** Serve in a Hot Chocolate cup.
- **For parcel:** Use a Hot Chocolate cup, cover with Black cap.
