# Hot Coffee (100ml)

## Ingredients:
- Nestlé coffee sachet (₹2) – 1 packet
- Milk – 100 ml
- Sugar – as required

## Method:
1. Take a pan and add coffee powder, milk, and sugar.
2. Boil it properly while stirring.
3. Garnish with a pinch of coffee powder and serve hot.

## Serving suggestions:
- **For dine-in:** Serve in a Hot Chocolate cup.
- **For parcel:** Use a Hot Chocolate cup, cover with Black cap.
