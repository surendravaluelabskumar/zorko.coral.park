# Veggie Mumbai Grilled Sandwich

## Ingredients:
- Jumbo Sandwich Bread – 3 slices
- Butter – 30 g
- Green Chutney – 30 g
- Tomato – 4–5 slices
- Cucumber – 4–5 slices
- Onion – 2–3 slices
- Capsicum – 2–3 slices
- Peri Peri Masala – as required
- Mashed Potato with Peri Peri & Garlic Paste – 80-100 g
- Barbeque Sauce – 20 g
- Global Sauce – 20 g

## Method:
1. Apply butter on all 3 slices of bread.
2. Spread green chutney evenly on each slice.
3. On the bottom slice, place tomato, cucumber, onion, and capsicum, then sprinkle peri peri masala and drizzle barbeque sauce in a zig-zag pattern.
4. Place the second slice on top and spread a layer of mashed potato mixed with peri peri and garlic butter, followed by a zig-zag drizzle of global sauce.
5. Place the third bread slice on top to complete the sandwich.
6. Apply butter on the outer sides of the sandwich and grill until golden brown and crisp.
7. Cut into 4 pieces and garnish with a little butter on top.

## Serving Style:
- **Dine-in:** Serve hot on ZORKO Butter Paper with green chutney dip.
- **Parcel:** Pack neatly in a Pizza Box with ZORKO Butter Paper and green chutney dip.
