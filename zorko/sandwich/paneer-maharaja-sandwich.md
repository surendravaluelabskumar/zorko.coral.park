# Paneer Maharaja Sandwich

## Ingredients:
- Sandwich Bread – 3 slices
- Butter – 30 g
- Green Chutney – 30 g
- Onion – 20 g (sliced)
- Tomato – 20 g (sliced)
- Cucumber – 20 g (sliced)
- Paneer – 50 g (cut into small cubes)
- Garlic Butter – 10 g
- Peri Peri Masala – 20 g
- Processed Cheese – 20 g (grated)

## Method:
1. Take 3 slices of sandwich bread and spread butter and green chutney evenly on all slices.
2. In a bowl, add paneer cubes, garlic butter, and peri peri masala. Mix well and microwave for 30 seconds.
3. On the bottom slice, place onion, tomato, and cucumber slices, sprinkle peri peri masala over it.
4. Place the second slice over it and spread the prepared paneer mixture evenly. Grate more cheese over the paneer.
5. Cover with the third slice of bread.
6. Apply butter on the outer sides and grill the sandwich until golden and crispy.
7. Cut into 4 triangle pieces and garnish with a little butter if desired.

## Serving Style:
- **For Dine-In:** Serve hot on ZORKO Butter Paper with green chutney dip.
- **For Parcel:** Pack neatly in a Pizza Box lined with ZORKO Butter Paper and green chutney dip.
