# Cheese Chilly Grill

## Ingredients:
- Sandwich Bread – 3 slices
- Butter – 30 g
- Green Chutney – 30 g
- Capsicum (finely chopped) – 80 g
- Green Chilli (finely chopped) – 10 g
- Peri Peri Powder – 5 g
- Salt – 2 g
- White Creamy Sauce – 50 g
- Processed Cheese – 20 g (grated)

## Method:
1. Take 3 slices of bread and apply butter and green chutney evenly on all of them.
2. In a bowl, mix finely chopped capsicum, green chili, a pinch salt, and white creamy sauce.
3. Spread the prepared capsicum mixture over one slice of bread and grate processed cheese on top.
4. Repeat the same mixture and cheese layer on the second slice.
5. Stack the third slice on top to complete the sandwich.
6. Grill the sandwich until golden and crisp in a sandwich griller.
7. Cut into 4 equal pieces.

## Serving Style:
- **For Dine-In:** Serve hot on ZORKO Butter Paper with green chutney dip.
- **For Parcel:** Pack neatly in a Pizza Box lined with ZORKO Butter Paper and green chutney dip.

**NOTE:** FOR CHEESE: GRATE AN EXTRA 20-25 G OF PROCESSED CHEESE ON TOP BEFORE SERVING.
