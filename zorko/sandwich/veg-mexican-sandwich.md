# Veg Mexican Sandwich

## Ingredients:
- Sandwich Bread – 3 slices
- Butter – 30 g
- Green Chutney – 30 g
- Cucumber – 4–5 slices
- Tomato – 4–5 slices
- Onion – 2–3 slices
- Capsicum – 2–3 slices
- Sweet Corn – as required
- Mashed Potato with Peri Peri and Garlic Butter – 125 g
- Orange Cheese Sauce – 10-15 g
- Global Sauce – 10-15 g
- Peri Peri Masala – 1 pinch

## Method:
1. Apply butter evenly on all three bread slices.
2. Spread green chutney on all three slices.
3. On the bottom slice, place tomato, cucumber, onion, capsicum, sweet corn, orange cheese sauce, and sprinkle peri peri masala and powder.
4. Cover with the second slice, then apply a layer of peri peri mashed potato with garlic butter and drizzle global sauce over it.
5. Place the third bread slice to complete the sandwich.
6. Apply butter on both outer sides and grill until golden brown and crisp.
7. Cut into 4 pieces and garnish the top with butter.

## Serving Style:
- **For Dine-In:** Serve hot on ZORKO Butter Paper with green chutney dip.
- **For Parcel:** Pack neatly in a Pizza Box lined with ZORKO Butter Paper and green chutney dip.
