# Cheese Garlic Bread

## Ingredients:
- Garlic Bread – 1 set (3 pcs)
- Garlic Butter – 25-30g (8-10gm each)
- White Creamy Sauce – 15-20g (5gm each)
- Processed Cheese – 25-30g (grated) (8-10gm each)
- Mozzarella Cheese – 15-20g (approx. 5- 8 g each)
- Pizza Seasoning – as required

## Method:
- Apply garlic butter evenly on each piece of bread.
- Drizzle white creamy sauce in a zig-zag manner covering the surface.
- Sprinkle grated processed cheese (about 8-10 g per piece).
- Add mozzarella cheese (5–8 g per piece) over the processed cheese.
- Bake at 180°C until the cheese melts and bread turns golden and crisp and Sprinkle pizza seasoning on top.

## Serving Style:
- **For Dine-in:** Serve hot on ZORKO Butter Paper.
- **For Parcel:** Pack in a Pizza Box lined with ZORKO Butter Paper.
