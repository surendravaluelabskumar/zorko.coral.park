# Supreme Treat Garlic Bread

## Ingredients:
- Garlic Bread – 1 set (3 pcs)
- Garlic Butter – 25 g (8-10gm each)
- White Creamy Sauce – 15-20g (5gm each)
- Processed Cheese – 25-30g (grated) (8-10gm each)
- Mozzarella Cheese – 15-20g (5-8 g each)
- Jalapeno – 9-10 pc (2-3 pcs each)
- Onion – 9-10 pc cube cut (2-3 pcs each)
- Sweet Corn – 10–15 pcs (5-6 pcs each)
- Pizza Seasoning – as required

## Method:
1. Take 3 pieces of garlic bread.
2. Apply garlic butter evenly on each slice.
3. Drizzle white creamy sauce in a zig-zag pattern.
4. Add grated processed cheese (about 8–10 g) on each slice.
5. Top with chopped onion, jalapeno, and sweet corn.
6. Add 5-8 g mozzarella cheese over the veggies.
7. Bake until the cheese melts and bread turn golden and crisp.
8. Sprinkle pizza seasoning on top.

## Serving Style:
- **For Dine-In:** Serve hot on ZORKO Butter Paper.
- **For Parcel:** Pack in a Pizza Box lined with ZORKO Butter Paper.
