# Paneer Toofani Garlic Bread

## Ingredients:
- Garlic Bread – 3 pcs
- Garlic Butter – 25 g (8-10 gm each)
- Green Chutney – 25 g
- Paneer – 30 g (small cubes)
- Onion – 20 g (diced)
- Salt – 2 g
- White creamy sauce – 15 gm (5g each)
- Processed Cheese – 25-30g (grated) (8-10gm each)
- Mozzarella Cheese – 15-20g (5-8 g each)
- Pizza Seasoning – 2 g

## Method:
1. Marinate paneer cubes with green chutney, diced onion, and salt.
2. Take garlic bread and apply garlic butter evenly on each piece.
3. Add white creamy sauce on it.
4. Sprinkle processed cheese over the bread.
5. Add the marinated paneer and onion mixture on top.
6. Add a layer of mozzarella cheese.
7. Bake until cheese melts and bread turn golden and crisp.
8. Sprinkle pizza seasoning on it.

## Serving Style:
- **For Dine-In:** Serve hot on ZORKO Butter Paper.
- **For Parcel:** Use a Pizza Box lined with ZORKO Butter Paper.
