# Cheese Chatori Kulhad Maggie

## Ingredients:
- Maggie Noodles – 1 pack
- Maggie Masala (from inside the packet)
- Magic Masala – 1 pack
- Processed Cheese – 20 g (mixed)
- Green Peas – as required
- Global Sauce – 20 g
- Barbeque Sauce – 20 g
- Mozzarella Cheese – 10-15 gm
- Kulhad – 1 no.
- Processed Cheese – 20-30 g

## Method:
1. In a vessel, bring water to a boil.
2. Add Maggie noodles and cook until 80% done.
3. Add the Maggie masala (from the packet) and magic masala and continue to cook.
4. Add green peas.
5. Stir in Barbeque Sauce (20 g) and Global Sauce (20 g).
6. Mix in 20 g of processed cheese and cook until it melts and blends into the noodles.
7. Wash and dry the kulhad properly.
8. Fill it with the prepared 1/3 of Chatori Maggie.
9. Top with the Processed cheese (10 g)
10. Repeat this 3 times.
11. Add 10-15 g mozzarella cheese on the top.
12. Microwave for 30 seconds and then bake it for 1.5-2 min.
13. Serve directly in the kulhad, hot and bubbling.

## Serving Suggestion:
- Serve hot in the kulhad cover with aluminum foil and a spoon.
