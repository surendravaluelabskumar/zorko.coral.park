# Cheese Volcano Kulhad Momos

## Ingredients:
- Momos – 3 pcs (fried)
- Mozzarella Cheese – 10-15gm
- White Creamy Sauce – 30-35 gm
- Global Sauce – 30-35 gm
- Barbeque Sauce – 30-35 gm
- Kulhad – 1 no.
- Processed Cheese 20-30 gm

## Method:
1. Microwave momos for 30-60 seconds, then deep fry and cut into halves (6 pieces).
2. Wash the kulhad.
3. In a kulhad, add a little white creamy sauce at the bottom.
4. Add 2 momo halves, drizzle all three sauces, add processed cheese.
5. Repeat the same layering two more times.
6. Top with mozzarella cheese and Microwave for 30 Seconds
7. Bake in oven at 180 degrees for 2 minutes till cheese melts.

## Serving Suggestion:
- Serve hot directly in the kulhad cover with aluminum foil and a spoon.
