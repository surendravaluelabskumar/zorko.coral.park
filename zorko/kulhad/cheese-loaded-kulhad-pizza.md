# Cheese Loaded Kulhad Pizza

## Ingredients:
- Butter – 10 g
- Capsicum – 20 g (chopped)
- Onion – 20 g (chopped)
- Sweet Corn – 10-12 pcs
- Paneer – 20-25 g (diced)
- Salt – as required
- Pizza Seasoning – 1 pinch
- Chilli Flakes – 2 pinches
- Pizza Sauce – 20 g
- White Creamy Sauce – 20 g
- Global Sauce – 20 g
- Half Pizza Base – cubed (same as paneer cubes)
- Processed Cheese – 20-30 g
- Mozzarella Cheese – 10-15 g
- Olives – 2-3 pcs (for garnish)

## Method:
### 1. Prepare the Filling:
- Heat a pan, add butter (10 g).
- Sauté onion (20 g), capsicum (20 g), sweet corn (10-12 pcs), and paneer (20–25 g).
- Season with salt, pizza seasoning, and chili flakes.
- Add Pizza Sauce (20 g), White Creamy Sauce (20 g), and Global Sauce (20 g).
- Add cubed pizza base and mix well.
### 2. Prepare the Kulhad:
- Wash and dry the kulhad.
- Add a little white creamy sauce + pinch of pizza seasoning at the base.
### 3. Layering in Kulhad:
- First Layer: Add a portion of the prepared mixture.
- Add 10-15 g grated processed cheese over this layer.
- Second Layer: Add another portion of the mixture.
- Again add 10-15 g grated processed cheese.
- Top Layer: Add last portion of the mixture
- Add 10-15 g mozzarella cheese on the top.
- Garnish with 2-3 olives.
### 4. Heating Process:
- First, microwave the kulhad for 30 seconds (to preheat and slightly melt the processed cheese).
- Then, bake in a preheated oven for 1.5-2 minutes until the mozzarella cheese on top melts and slightly bubbles.

## Serving Suggestion:
- Serve hot in the kulhad cover with aluminum foil and a spoon.
